About SRE ITPLN

Society of Renewable Energy Institut Teknologi PLN

Empowering the future through sustainable energy innovation

SRE ITPLN is a student organization dedicated to advancing renewable energy knowledge and technology within our campus community. We believe in creating a sustainable future through education, innovation, and practical applications of renewable energy solutions.
